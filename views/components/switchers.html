Small switcher

Smaller verstion of the switcher. Add .switcher--sm modifier class to the .switcher class. Add .switcher__label--sm modifier class to the .switcher__label class.

<div class="switcher switcher--sm">
  <input type="checkbox" class="switcher__input" id="switcher-sm-demo">
  <label class="switcher__label switcher__label--sm" for="switcher-sm-demo">
    <div class="switcher__inner">
      <div class="switcher__on"><i class="ficon ficon-checkmark"></i></div>
      <div class="switcher__off"><i class="ficon ficon-cross"></i></div>
    </div>
  </label>
</div>
